Marine Transportation

------------------------------

International shipping can reach Angola via the ports of Luanda, Lobito, Namibe and Cabinda. No organized river-borne passenger transport companies operate in Angola today. Shipments bound for Angolan ports require a Certificado de Embarque, the loading certificate, to unload cargo upon arrival. It is the responsibility of the shipper to obtain the document before the shipment leaves for Angola from the Conselho Naciona
