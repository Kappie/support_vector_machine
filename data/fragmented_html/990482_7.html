The condensed histogram H' may be interpreted as follows: H'(I) = H(I) + H(I+1) + ... + H(I+M-1) for I=1, M+1, 2*M+1, 3*M+1,... H'(I) = 0 for all other I. After zeroing out the excluded elements from the histogram, the mean and standard deviation computed from the excluded histogram are printed out.
