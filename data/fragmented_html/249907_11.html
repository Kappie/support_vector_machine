IRB Review of the Protocol and Informed Consent

Once the final SR/NSR decision has been rendered by the IRB (or FDA), the IRB must consider whether or not the study should be approved. In considering whether a study should be approved, the IRB should use the same criteria it would use in considering approval of any research involving an FDA regulated product [21 CFR 56.111].
