To examine the effects of nonnutritive suckling and intraoral sucrose on CFA-induced Fos-like immunoreactivity (Fos-LI), pups were divided into two groups. Rats in one group suckled their anesthetized mother and received intermittent sucrose infusion on a 10-min-on/10-min-off cycle.
