A. If a MEWA is not itself an ERISA-covered plan, ERISA’s preemption provisions do not limit the ability of states to regulate the arrangement in accordance with any applicable state insurance law. Thus, unless the Bend Chamber arrangement is itself an ERISA-covered employee benefit plan, ERISA would impose no limit on the application of state insurance law to the arrangement.
