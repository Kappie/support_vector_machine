Research into Gastric Cancer

Background

Helicobacter pylori infection is causal for non-cardia gastric cancer. Independently, the Branch discovered that non-cardia gastric cancer risk was significantly increased with polymorphisms in pro-inflammatory variants in the IL-1 gene cluster.
