Families were interviewed annually from 1968 to 1997, and every other year since 1997. The interview, which averaged 72 minutes in 2003, is completed by telephone for 97 percent of the families and face-to-face for the remaining 3 percent. Expenditures are reported for the family as a whole, where a PSID family is defined as a group of people living together as a family.
