The ratio of the normal stress change to the right-lateral shear stress change ranges from ~1:1 to ~1.8:1 in the seismically active parts of the southern Hayward fault. Thus if a frictional failure criterion is applied with intermediate to high friction (m=0.6-0.8), the overall effect is neutral or slightly discouraging to seismicity.
