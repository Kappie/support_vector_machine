The case described here had to be reported to the judicial authority (as established by the Italian Penal Code) and was referred to the Italian Workers' Compensation Authority, which recognized the occupational origin of the disease.

After the correct diagnosis was formulated, the patient was treated with cytoreductive surgery associated with continuous hyperthermic peritoneal perfusion (CHHP) and survived 23 months.
