   CALL CGV2EL ( CENTER, V1, V2, ELLIPS )
This call produces the SPICELIB ellipse ELLIPS, which represents the same mathematical ellipse as do CENTER, V1, and V2.

The generating vectors need not be linearly independent. If they are not, the resulting ellipse will be degenerate. Specifically, if the generating vectors are both zero, the ellipse will be the single point represented by CENTER, and if just one of the semi-axis vector
