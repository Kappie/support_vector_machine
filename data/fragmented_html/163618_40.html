R. 1252, Judicial Reform Act of 1997
The House Judiciary Subcommittee on Courts and Intellectual Property held a hearing on H.R. 1252, Judicial Reform Act of 1997, and reported the bill with amendments to the full Judiciary Committee for consideration in the second session of Congress.
