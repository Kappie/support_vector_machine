  • Male Fischer F344 rats were given citral labelled with 14C at the C1 and C2 positions in a single oral dose of 5, 50, or 500 mg/kg bw or an intravenous dose of 5 mg/kg bw. After 72 h, the animals were sacrificed and tissues and excreta analyzed for radioactivity. Most radiolabel was excreted in the urine, feces, and expired air as 14CO2 or [14C]citral within 24 hr, regardless of the dose
