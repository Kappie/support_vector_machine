Other Agents

Many other agents have been reported in small trials or case reports as having shown efficacy in treating patients with cluster headaches. These include clonidine, diltiazem, flunarizine, somatostatin, indomethacin, pizotifen, phenelzine, naratriptan, gabapentin, methylphenidate, mirtazapine, tizanidine, and baclofen.
