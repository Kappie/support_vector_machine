However, at the urging of the 10th Circuit Judges' Restoration Committee, the General Services Administration acquired the building in 1988 and restoration was begun. The subsequent restoration was so faithful to original drawings and historical photos that the courthouse received the 1994 Modernization Award from Buildings Magazine
