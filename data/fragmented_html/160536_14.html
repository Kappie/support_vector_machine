Within a day or two an electronic distance meter (EDM) arrived, and reflectors were placed on the mountain. The EDM is a sophisticated instrument, but the targets were nothing more than clear plastic highway reflectors screwed to boards that were lashed to steel fence posts hammered into the ground.
