If the sponsor believes the device does not impart significant risk, IRB approval of a study as an NSR device can be sought. The NSR category was created to avoid delay and expense where the anticipated risk to human subjects did not justify the involvement of FDA. If the IRB agrees that the study is NSR, no submission to or review by FDA is necessary before starting studies
