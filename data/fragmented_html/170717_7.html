Collections of Fragaria chiloensis, a parent of the cultivated strawberry is being exploited as a source of valuable traits, including a high capacity for photosynthesis, growth habits, cold hardiness, stress tolerance, and pest resistance. A management strategy is being developed to maintain and improve trait quality
