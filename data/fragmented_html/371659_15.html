B ellipse or make an assignment from an element of a SPICELIB ellipse. The contents of SPICELIB ellipses are considered an implementation choice, and are not described by the specification of any SPICELIB routine. In the future, the definition of the contents of SPICELIB ellipses may be changed in any way that does not affect the user-visible behavior of the access routines.
