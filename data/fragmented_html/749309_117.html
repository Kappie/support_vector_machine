The Association of Universities for Research in Astronomy (AURA) is sponsoring a workshop to engage the UV/optical/Near-IR (UVOIR) astronomy community to discuss its long-term goals for space-based astronomy and astrophysics. The meeting will provide the community with an opportunity to look forward on a 25-year horizon to identify the scientific opportunities enabled by large and very large space telescopes and to outline a path forward that will form the basis for a community-led repo
