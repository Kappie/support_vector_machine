Interpretive Summary: This report describes the isolation and characterization of a new type of virus found in a wild pronghorn antelope. The virus is distantly related to viruses, such as bovine viral diarrhea virus (BVDV) and classical swine fever virus (CSFV, also known as hog cholera virus), that cause major economic losses to cattle and hog producers.
