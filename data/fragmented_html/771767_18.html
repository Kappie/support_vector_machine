Intensive culture may be a better alternative for hatcheries that lack the pond facilities or sites for cage culture. In intensive culture of walleye, a single interval of high mortality occurs during the critical period when fish switch from yolk-sac nutrition (endogenous feeding) to active feeding (exogenous feeding), which coincides with the interval of gas bladder inflation.
