Prednisone is usually initiated at a dose of 40 to 60 mg per day for 3 days, followed by a 5 to 10 mg dosage reduction every 2 to 3 days until discontinued. Dexamethasone 4 mg twice a day for 2 weeks, then 4 mg per day for 1 week, is also effective.
