Commissioner of the Social Security Administration, their successors in office, their agents, employees and all persons acting in concert with them are enjoined in the Ninth Circuit from using nonrecurring income received in the first month of SSI eligibility or reeligibility to determine any more than one month's benefit in the first three month's of eligibility.
