mon allusion from mythology is the term "Pandora's Box". If a person says, he is afraid to bring up a subject because it would be like opening Pandora's Box, he is referring to the myth of Pandora. According to the myth because of her curiosity, Pandora opened a box from which all the evils of mankind escaped, causing all the problems of the world. Thus, the term has come to imply disorder, chaos, and a sense of evil foreboding.
