However, an alternative approach may be to measure the prevalence of adults who have no known risk factors for CHD. This report provides state-specific estimates of and characterizes adults who report having no known major risk factors for CHD.

Data were analyzed from 91,428 persons aged greater than or equal to 18 years who resided in 48 states and the District of Columbia
