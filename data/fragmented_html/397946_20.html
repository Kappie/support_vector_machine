Of the 19 storms that were not associated with a line, 12 occurred in a storm cluster, six were fairly isolated with one being truly isolated.  Of the 12 that occurred within a cluster, seven were on the cluster’s south end, four were at the cluster’s east end and one was at the cluster’s west end.  Five of the six fairly isolated storms had other storms away from the storm
