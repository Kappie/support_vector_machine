The effectiveness of the larvae in this blind search is borne out by research in which one individual was observed to have captured three aphids in one minute when the researcher removed the captive aphid by forceps each time one was caught. Normally, 3 minutes are required between captures

The predaceous syrphid flies also have their problems in this complex food web and many of them end up as food for the larvae
