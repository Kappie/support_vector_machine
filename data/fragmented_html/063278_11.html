s="report_peggy">The industry standard measure for oil density is API gravity; a lower gravity indicates higher density (heavy viscous oil), and a higher gravity indicates lower density (lighter, thinner oil). Over the past 20 years, the API gravity of imported crude oil has steadily declined, from 32.5 degrees to 30.2 degrees [27].
