Road Transportation

--------------------------

Of 72,000 kilometers of road, less than 20,000 is paved. Even some of the paved roads are unusable due to downed bridges and landmines, though most of the major roads linking provincial capitals have been demined. The general condition of all roads and related infrastructure is poor, even within the city limits of Luanda. Gasoline is available in most urban areas.
