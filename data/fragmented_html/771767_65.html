n="left">Turbid water culture

Survival, length, and weight of larvae raised in turbid water to 21-30 days were significantly greater than for larvae raised in clear water. Larvae were more evenly distributed in the turbid water than in clear water. The turbid water was milky gray. In turbid water, larvae did not congregate on the tank walls as they did in clear water.
