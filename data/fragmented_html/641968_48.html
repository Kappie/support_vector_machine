Mountains spotted nine men carrying large bundles of marijuana, one of whom also had a rifle in his hands. As the agents moved in to apprehend the subjects, the group dropped the bundles and the rifle and fled in different directions. Three of the men who attempted to abscond were caught, along with all of the drugs and the rifle. The marijuana, weighing over 392 pounds and an SKS rifle were seized.
