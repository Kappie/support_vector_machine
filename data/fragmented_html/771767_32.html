>Bulkowski and Meade (1983) described a behavioral change in juvenile walleye from positive phototaxis to negative phototaxis behavior when they are 32 to 40 mm. In intensive culture, this change begins in juveniles as early as 25 mm. The changing response of juveniles to light is important in both extensive and intensive culture.
