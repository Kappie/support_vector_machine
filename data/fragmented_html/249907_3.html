Federal requirements governing investigations involving medical devices were enacted as part of the Medical Device Amendments of 1976 and the Safe Medical Devices Act of 1990. These amendments to the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (the Act) define the regulatory framework for medical device development, testing, approval, and marketing.
