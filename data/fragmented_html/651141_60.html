Because the PSID expanded the set of consumption expenditure questions in 1999, it now provides a fairly accurate picture of most household consumption expenditure and is thus an even more useful resource for studying consumption. For example, combined with the PSID's detailed income data, estimates of the effect of income on expenditures can now be derived for a variety of expenditure categories.
