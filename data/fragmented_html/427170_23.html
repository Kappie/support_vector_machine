Toxicity was confined to decreases in body weight at the 10% concentration in mice, at the 5 and 10% concentrations in rats, and decreases in absolute weights of the liver, kidney and spleen at the 10% concentration in rats. The only histopathological change observed was minimal to mild hyperplasia and/or squamous metaplasia of the respiratory epithelium in the anterior portion of the nasal passages of rats fed 5 or 10% citral microcapsules.
