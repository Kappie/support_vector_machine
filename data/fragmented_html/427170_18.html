  • SURVEILLANCE: A survey of sensitization data from tests on materials containing citral was conducted under the auspices of the Soap and Detergent Association (SDA). This survey was restricted to skin patch tests on human subjects conducted in the USA by member companies of SDA and by perfume suppliers.
