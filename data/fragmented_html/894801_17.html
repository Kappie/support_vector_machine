Moreover, the predictions of the LB model must be considered with caution when physically relevant scales in physical problems become smaller than this coarse-graining scale (lattice spacing) of the LB lattice model. This limitation is natural since the LB model is a mesoscopic description of a fluid rather than a microscopic model.
