Material eluting in the position expected for cyclic β-1,6-β-1,3-glucan was pooled and subsequently analyzed by thin-layer chromatography (TLC) using aluminum-backed Silica Gel 60 plates (EM Industries, Gibbstown, N.J.) and a butanol-ethanol-water (5:5:4) solvent system. Samples were visualized on TLC plates by charring at 170°C for 20 min after spraying with 5% sulfuric acid in methanol (vol/vol) (7).
