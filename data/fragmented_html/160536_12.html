The North-Flank Bulge

In mid-April, 10-12 days after monitoring of the lake began, several different geologists viewing the volcano noticed that its upper north flank had apparently deformed relative to its pre-1980 shape. Earthquakes recorded on portable seismographs were located beneath the north flank, so it seemed reasonable that the deformation was related to shallow intrusion of magma into the edifice.
