Subpart D_Landing Requirements Sec. 122.38 Permit and special license to unlade and lade. (a) Applicability. Before any passengers, baggage, or merchandise may be unladen or laden aboard on arrival or departure of an aircraft subject to these regulations, a permit and/or special license to unlade or lade shall be obtained from Customs.
