Radiofrequency Thermal Neurolysis of the Trigeminal Nerve

Radiofrequency thermal neurolysis of the trigeminal nerve is a potential treatment choice. Results vary, but 50 to 73.3% of 30 patients described 50 to 90% relief from their cluster headache
