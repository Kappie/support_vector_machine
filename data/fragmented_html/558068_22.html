Scenario 2. If your kVp meter is not manufacturer-certified for W/Ag and W/Rh beams and you are using a mammography-calibrated ion chamber, you will need to include a correction factor in your kVp measurements. This correction factor may be obtained from the kVp meter manufacturer or by working with the mammography unit’s service engineer (by comparing the engineer’s manufacturer-approved measurements during machine installation with your noninvasive meter results)
