Compressors should not be used to aerate water destined for use in intensive culture of fry because they often contaminate the air with oil in the compression cycle. Compressed air contaminated with oil that is bubbled through water will transfer the oil to the water. The oil will rise to the surface and interfere with gas bladder inflation.
