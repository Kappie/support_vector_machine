How did Nordhaus arrive at climate damage being about 1 percent of GNP? He assumed that agriculture was the most vulnerable economic market sector to climate change. For decades agronomists had calculated potential changes to crop yields from various climate change scenarios, suggesting some regions now too hot would sustain heavy losses from warming whereas others, now too cold, could gain.
