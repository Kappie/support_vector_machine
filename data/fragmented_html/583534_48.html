Lariam/Mefloquine, Malarone, and Doxycyline are commonly used malaria prophylaxis drugs that must be taken continuously, starting before arrival in Luanda. The Angolan government requires a yellow fever vaccination. Those who come without proof of vaccination risk on-the-spot vaccination at the airport in Luanda.
