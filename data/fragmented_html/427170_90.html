At the lowest oral dose, 83% of the radiolabel was recovered within 72 hr (51% in urine, 12% in feces, 17% as expired 14CO2, <1% as expired [14C]citral, and 3% in total tissues). Production of 14CO2 essentially ceased 12 hr after treatment, and the amount of 14C found in any tissue was very small (<2%).
