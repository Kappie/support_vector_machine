Clinging of fry to the sidewalls of the tank will affect success of the culture system. The clinging behavior is a function of tank size, color of the tank walls, light intensity, and turbidity. Fry are strongly attracted to light, direct or reflected light. They will cling to the sidewalls, screens, and they are even attracted to the bottom in tanks with black sides but light colored, bottom.
