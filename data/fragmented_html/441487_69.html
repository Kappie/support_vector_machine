Even so, they can build up rapidly in several mild years until the valley again rings with the territorial calls, and most sequoias are again having their cones cut. Not for long, however: an outbreak of plague, a long, cold winter, or heavier than normal predation, and down go their populations as the forest's complex ecological relationships bring them into bounds.</
