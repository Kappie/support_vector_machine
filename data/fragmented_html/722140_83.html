The first reports on the carcinogenic effect of asbestos on the lung date back to 1935 (Gloyne 1935; Lynch and Smith 1935), whereas asbestos-related mesotheliomas, both pleural (Wagner et al. 1960) and peritoneal (Keal 1960), were first described in 1960.
