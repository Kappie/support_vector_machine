H.R. 875, S. 394, Judicial Compensation

H.R. 875, a bill to adjust, and provide a procedure for the future adjustment of, the salaries of federal judges was introduced in the House by Representatives Henry J. Hyde (R-IL) and John Conyers (D-MI). The bill collected 89 co-sponsors, but did not move out of the House Judiciary Subcommittee on Courts and Intellectual Property.
