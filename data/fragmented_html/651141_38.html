The life-cycle profiles for rent are nearly identical in the PSID and CE. Mortgage payments and home insurance are higher in the PSID, while utilities and property taxes are somewhat higher in the CE. Altogether, housing expenditures increase through the late thirties or early forties, and then begin a rapid decline after the mid-fifties, with consistent patterns in the two surveys.
