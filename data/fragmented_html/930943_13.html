Background: When maximal viral suppression is the goal of HAART, drug regimens become complex and more toxic. The study objective was to seek differences in clinical course between patients whose HIV is maximally, and sub-maximally suppressed. Methods: A practice with 600 HIV patients has been part of a large observational database.
