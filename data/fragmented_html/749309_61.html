The School of Physics at the Georgia Institute of Technology is pleased to announce the inaugural conference of the Center for Relativistic Astrophysics (CRA): Multi-Messenger Relativistic Astrophysics 2009. The newly established CRA is devoted to interdisciplinary research and education in multi-messenger astrophysics, linking high energy, particle and gravitational astrophysics.
