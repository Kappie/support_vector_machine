Similar to the approach used by Fisher and Johnson (2006) and Skinner (1987) to impute consumption, we impute total PSID expenditure from a regression model estimated with CE data where explanatory variables include expenditures in each category measured in the PSID. The R-squared from the imputation model was 0.88. The estimates from the model are reported in Table A3.
